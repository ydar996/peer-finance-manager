Assurance Investment and Cooperative Inc. (AIC) is a member-owned cooperative society dedicated to promoting financial independence and mutual support through regular contributions and shared investments.

Mission

We help members access funds at lower interest rates than traditional banks while building wealth together through disciplined saving and investment.

Overview

AIC provides access to passive income through real estate and diversified investments. Membership is open to all; members attend meetings and commit financially to the cooperative.

Membership

  • One-time intake deposit: $100
  • Minimum monthly contribution: $50 (installments of $50–$500, up to $3,000 per month)
  • Sign the cooperative bylaws and participate in meetings and voting
  • Volunteer on committees when called upon

Goals

Immediate: Invest member contributions in Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) that provide regular passive income.

Short term (6–12 months): Begin member lending based on contributions; pursue a property for resale; explore bulk purchasing for members; evaluate additional passive-income opportunities.

Long term: Expand into stocks, bonds, and other instruments; partner with other cooperatives and financial institutions; support emergency loans for real estate transactions.

Benefits of Joining

  • Ownership and control — members have a voice in how the cooperative is run
  • Access to financial services — savings, loans, and shared investment opportunities
  • Diversification — pooled resources across a range of assets
  • Lower costs — not-for-profit structure supports competitive fees and rates
  • Community — regular meetings build relationships and shared purpose
  • Education and support — financial literacy and peer learning
  • Building wealth — long-term security through disciplined accumulation
