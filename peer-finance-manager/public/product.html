Capabilities

Everything a growing cooperative needs in one place

From daily deposits to year-end reporting, designed for treasurers who wear many hats and members who deserve transparency.

📊

Cooperative Books dashboard

Live summary cards for contributions, expenses, distributions, loan interest, CD balances, and bank cash. Click any card to drill into member-level detail.

👥

Member accounts & profiles

Search by name or member number. Each profile shows contribution history, loan schedules, biodata, and downloadable PDF statements.

🏦

Bank import & ledger

Import your master Excel ledger aligned to real bank statements. Catch balance mismatches before they become reconciliation nightmares.

💳

Loans & repayments

Create loans with guarantors, eligibility rules, repayment schedules, and portfolio views. Members see their own loan activity in My Account.

📝

Record & import

Enter contributions, withdrawals, expenses, distributions, and registrations, or bulk-import spreadsheets and membership applications.

📄

Statements & reports

Batch monthly contribution statements, per-loan PDFs, and auto-generated cooperative performance reports you can publish to members.

📅

Meetings & announcements

Schedule meetings, share agendas and virtual links, and notify members when email is configured.

Public About & Bylaws pages

Typical workflow

From signup to month-end report in four steps

  1. Register your cooperative Create your organization, choose a code, and set up the first administrator.
  2. Add members & import history Enter biodata, link ledger names, and import your bank ledger or spreadsheet.
  3. Run day-to-day from Cooperative Books Record deposits, loan repayments, and expenses, or import new bank activity.
  4. Publish to members Statements, performance reports, and meeting notices appear in each member’s portal.

Built for trust

Each cooperative is isolated and secure

Every organization has its own data space. Members only see their own accounts. Staff can review without editing. Subscription billing keeps the platform sustainable while your cooperative keeps full control of its books.

  • Role-based access (member, staff, admin)
  • Organization code at sign-in
  • Stripe card billing or check payment
  • Grace period when payment lapses: view continues
  • Automated month-end report generation
