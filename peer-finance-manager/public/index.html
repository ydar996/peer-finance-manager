    Organization & Report Settings

    Default is Pacific (Los Angeles / San Diego). All administrators share one time zone per Cooperative.

    Month-end auto-generate and auto-publish are on by default (scheduler runs every 6 hours on the server). On the last day of each month the Cooperative report is built as at that calendar month-end date and published to members. You can still regenerate and publish manually anytime.
